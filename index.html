Make every food ingredients count

SnapDish is an innovative application designed to streamline meal preparation by providing food recommendations and recipes based on ingredients users have on hand. Through advanced image processing and manual input, SnapDish can quickly identify ingredients and provide top 3 most suitable food recommendations based on identified ingredients. By delivering a practical tool to mitigate food waste, SnapDish aims to empower users to maximize their ingredients while promoting environmentally responsible behaviors.

Object Detection

SnapDish can detect food ingredients from a picture by using machine learning. It will scan multiple objects in a picture and produce a list of ingredients as an output.

Natural Language Processing

SnapDish will give you recipe recommendation based on ingredients detected.
